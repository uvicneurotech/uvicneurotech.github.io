Neurotechnology Innovation at the University of Victoria

About

UVIC Neurotech is a club located at the University of Victoria in Canada. Our club was created to inspire students at the University of Victoria to explore neurotechnology. We like to inspire students by showing them that neurotechnology is accessible to students by becoming a part of NeuroTechX and their annual competition.

Projects

We don't have any projects completed yet but when we do complete our first project we will be posting it here. Our projects will mainly be focused around the annual NeuroTechX competition. The NeuroTechX competition consists of an open challenge and a fixed challenge. The fixed challenge is about building an EEG and aquiring brain signals. However, the open challenge allows teams to come up with their own neurotechnology project. The NeuroTechX open competition is where UVIC Neurotech really hopes to shine and create some truly innovative technology.

Contact

If you would like to contact us regarding a question about the club, or if you would like to join, you can email us at uvicneurotech@gmail.com. We have people from biomedical engineering, biology, software engineering, computer science, and electrical engineering working together. However, we encourage everyone to join.
